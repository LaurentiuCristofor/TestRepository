Conclusion

Creating an ebook using Calibre is a pretty simple process. The most important steps are to plan the chapters of your book, then to create the XHTML files for holding their content. The remainder of the work will consist just of the plain work of editing your content and of updating the Table of Contents and the content.opf file whenever you add new content. And don't forget to also regenerate the logical Table of Contents at the end.
